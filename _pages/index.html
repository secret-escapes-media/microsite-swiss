The snow-capped peaks of the Swiss Alps provide a winter-sports paradise, making Switzerland a premiere ski destination for hurtling down white-powder pistes.

From alpine tradition to modern urban flair, Switzerland seamlessly intertwines old and new, catering for a vast range of tastes. Snowy chalets and cosy mountain pubs, cable cars and cuckoo clocks – tropes all found on the mountainside, sat next to bustling cities and chic ski resorts packed with contemporary art, fine dining, boutique shopping and trendy rooftop bars. If your winter dream is sailing down mountainsides and winding down in a hot tub, heading out and exploring your decadent apres-ski options, make Switzerland this year’s ski destination.
Why Ski in Switzerland?

As the snow falls heavy over the iconic peaks of the Alps, Switzerland turns into a veritable winter-sports playground. With some of the most impressive mountains in the range, plus the most luxurious ski resorts you’ll find anywhere, Switzerland is a superlative choice for any ski holiday this winter.

With SWISS offering free ski equipment transport*, plus tickets from as little as £74 one-way*, you’ll be treated to warm, Swiss hospitality before you’ve even landed. Even more reason to grab your skis and jet off to Switzerland.
